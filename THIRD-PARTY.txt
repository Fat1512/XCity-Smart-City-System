2. The HelthyAir Dataset: Outdoor Air Quality in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
   - Version: 1
   - Published: 3 October 2022
   - DOI: 10.17632/pk6tzrjks8.1
   - Contributors: Rajnish Rakholia, Quan Le, Bang Ho, Khue Vu, Ricardo Simon
   - License: CC BY 4.0
   - Description: 
     Outdoor air quality data from six monitoring stations in HCMC. Includes PM2.5, TSP, SO2, O3, NO2, CO, Temperature, Humidity.
   - Usage: Air quality modeling, spatiotemporal analysis, assessment across traffic, residential, industrial areas
   - Download: Air Quality Ho Chi Minh City.csv (4.54 MB)
